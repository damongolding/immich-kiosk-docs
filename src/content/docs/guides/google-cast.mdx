## Using Kiosk on Google Cast Devices

Create a photo slideshow in Home Assistant and display it on any Google Cast device (like Chromecast or Nest Hub).

Follow this community guide writtend by Chris Burrow to get started: [Immich Kiosk Mode](https://github.com/chris-burrow-apps/home-assistant-blueprints/blob/main/immich/immich_kiosk_mode.md)
